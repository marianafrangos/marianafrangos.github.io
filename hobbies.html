Greek Dance

I've been Greek dancing since I was about five years old. I've learned dances from regions in Greece such as Crete, Sarakatsani, Evvia, Sfakia, Agrafa. Almost every year, I compete with my Greek dance team against other Greek dancers all across the West Coast at a competiton called FDF. This past year, my team did dances from Agrafa and Sarakatsani, and we won first place in the Senior Division. I love Greek dance because my culture is important to me and I believe it is a priviledge to be able to keep these traditions alive.

Running

Running clears my mind and keeps me grounded. Last year, I ran my first 10k. Since recovering from an ankle injury, I am now training for my first half marathon.

Baking and Charcuterie

I began baking when I was only three years old, helping my mom make banana bread. I still enjoy baking today as an adult because it comforts me and creates connections with the people I am close with. I also recently have discovered how much I enjoy making elaborate charcuterie boards, and I enjoy sharing those with my friends as well.
